Who is Michele?

I'm a full-stack data nerd working in visualisation, analytics, engineering and solution architecture. I believe in the power of data as a force for change in our lives and the world around us. My professional passion is helping clients to plan and implement data strategies to enable their teams to embrace data-driven decision making.

Based mainly in Lisbon and part-time in Cape Town and Atlanta, I work with teams all around the world. When I'm not catching flights, I enjoy a swim in the icy Atlantic, skating by the seaside, surfing, dinner parties, craft beer, a good bagel and petting all the friendly dogs.
